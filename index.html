A little about Mike

Mike Recachinas is an entrepreneurial electrical engineering and computer science double major with a passion for web development.

Mike loves getting his hands dirty in some low-level languages, but he has a soft spot for high-level languages too. He has experience in x86 assembly, C, C++, Python, Ruby, Java, JavaScript, CoffeeScript, and most recently Haskell. His interests include big data, security, machine learning, and front end design.

His electrical engineering interests include optics and lasers, VLSI, and microwaves/RF design.
