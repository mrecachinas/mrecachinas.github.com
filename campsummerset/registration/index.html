Cancellation & Refund Policy

Families may request a refund of Tuition and Fees any time up to 2 weeks prior to the start of a Camp Session. Families must inform Camp Summerset in writing or by email that a refund is requested. Upon receipt of this written request, Tuition and Fees will be refunded, minus a $50 administrative/cancellation fee. Tuition and Fees will be refunded within 2 weeks of receipt of written request.

*Please note that refunds will not be provided in the event that camp is closed due to weather or other emergencies.

Forms

All forms MUST be submitted before your child begins his or her first day.
  • Proof of up-to-date immunizations must be on file with Camp Summerset before a camper can start.
  • This is separate from any immunizations on file with your child's elementary school (Somerset E.S. or any other) or MCPS.
  • We ask that you get your child's immunization records to us no later than two weeks before the start of camp.
  • There are several ways to get these records to us:
    1. If your child attended Camp Summerset in 2013, and your child's records were up to date, they may still be up to date. Contact Greg Matwey to inquire.
    2. If your child's immunization records are on file with your child's school, you may be able to visit the school's main office to ask the nurse to borrow the records, photocopy the records and send to Camp Summerset Ask your doctor for a copy of your child's records. These may be faxed to Camp Summerset or mailed.
    3. Please note that it normally takes your doctor up to 10 business days to process a request for records.

All forms are in PDF form. Additionally, we've added a ZIP file containing all the forms!
