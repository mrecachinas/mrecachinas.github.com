Why should YOU choose Camp Summerset?

Camp Summerset offers FIVE kinds of experiences depending on the age of your child!

Minnows (age 4)
MiniCamp (rising kindergarteners)
Classic Camp Juniors (grades 1 & 2)
Classic Camp Seniors (grades 3 - 6)
CityVentures (rising 4-6 graders)

See our PROGRAMS page for more info!

When is Camp?

Camp Summerset offers three 2 week sessions.

Session 1 : June 16 - June 27, 2014
Session 2 : June 30 - July 11, 2014 (closed July 4)
Session 3 : July 14 - July 25, 2014

Hours

Regular Camp Hours: 9am to 3pm
Drop-off in the morning: 8:45am to 9am
Pick-up in the afternoon: 2:50pm to 3pm

Before/After Care

Before Care : 7:45am to 8:45am
$100 per 10-day session (if pre-arranged at the start of each session)
$15 per day, drop-in (if not pre-arranged)

After Care : 3pm to 6pm
$250 per 10-day session (if pre-arranged at the start of each session)
$30 per day, drop-in (if not pre-arranged)

Combined Before Care and After Care
$300 for each 10-day session
