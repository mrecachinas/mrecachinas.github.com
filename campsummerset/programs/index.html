Minnows

Camp Summerset's newest camp for Pre-K, 4-year olds!

Camp Summerset offers a self-contained camp for children who are four-years old. The Minnows will enjoy a theme-based curriculum featuring a variety of fun-filled activities while making new friends. These fun activities include...

  • Storytelling
  • Creative Dramatics
  • Camp Games
  • Arts & Crafts
  • and Water Play!

The Minnows will eat a bag lunch at camp before going home. Minnows Camp hours are 9:00 am - 1:00 pm. Campers will need to bring a bag lunch, a drink, a snack, change of clothes, towel, and bathing suit to camp each day. All of these items must be labeled with the child's name and sent in a backpack or tote.

Don't Wait!

Sign up early for this wonderful experience for your 4 year old. Spaces are limited. For more information, please contact the camp director.

Mini Camp

For soon-to-be Kindergarteners

Camp Summerset Mini Camp is a full-day camp (9:00 am to 3:00 pm) for children who are four or five years old and entering kindergarten! Mini Camp introduces your child to the school/camp environment. Campers remain primarily with one teacher while participating in a variety of camp activities, such as...

  • arts & crafts
  • storytelling & drama
  • science
  • music
  • sports & games
  • water play
  • and other indoor and outdoor activities!

With parental permission, Mini Campers go swimming at the Somerset Pool four times per session!

Sign up today!

Space will be on a first-come, first-served basis. A limited number of slots are available. All Mini Campers will be together throughout the day so you are encouraged to sign up with family, friends, and neighbors!

CityVentures

Our NEWEST camp!

Accompanied by two teachers, campers entering grades 4 - 6 set off on an exciting series of explorations in and around our nation's capital.

  • Museums, monuments, parks, and trails!
  • World famous attractions and hidden gems!

Campers safely navigate public transportation and city streets as they sample what the city has to offer. During each session, 5 days of travel are interspersed with engaging follow-up activities. Let’s have a “CityVenture”!
