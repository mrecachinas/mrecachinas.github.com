Mr. Matwey's Message

Welcome to Camp Summerset, your favorite whole-kid-focused neighborhood camp!

I was delighted to serve as Co-Director for the first time last year and it couldn't have been a better year. Thank you to all the campers, parents, families, and staff that made it such a wonderful experience.

In 2013 Camp Summerset was pleased to offer great new classes and welcome fantastic new teachers. Camp Summerset 2014 will continue that tradition. We are excited to announce the addition of Magic and Spanish (Fiestas y Viajes) and new teachers, including Somerset's own first grade teachers, Hannah Rich and Yuna Choi.

As always, we will offer five programs for children aged 4 to 11, including Mini Camp, Minnows, and City Ventures, in addition to Classic Camp (Juniors and Seniors). Our returning classes, including Tennis, Computers, Music, Yoga, Science, Sports, and more, make up our signature mix of classes designed to stimulate kids' bodies, minds, creativity, and imagination. Finally, we will once again offer Before Care and After Care to suit the needs of working families.

We hope you will join us this year for our 21st summer of excitement, explorations, and fun. We look forward to seeing you!

Sincerely,
Greg Matwey

From the Desk of Mrs. K

Hello Campers! I cannot wait to see all of you at Camp Summerset this summer! We are going to have another exciting and fun summer together. Our Classic Campers will begin their day with our morning meeting at 9:00, where we will share news, the lucky basket, songs, and jokes and riddles. In some of our new classes, Mr. Ira will help turn you into a magician in his Magic class - or a toymaker in his Puppetry class, and Ms. Morgan's Music classes will bring "Broadway" to us and give you a chance to try your music trivia knowledge with "Name That Tune". Visit our list of classes and read about the rest of our exciting and creative choices. We also want to welcome Minnows and MiniCampers to our camp for lots and lots of fun!! Our teachers, counselors, CIT's and I will be looking for your smiling faces this summer.

Looking forward to Camp Summerset 2014,
Ms. K
